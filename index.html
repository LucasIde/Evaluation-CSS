Our Work

A Legacy of Winning

Hicks Thomas LLP is a litigation boutique with extensive expertise across a wide range of industries. We represent both plaintiffs and defendants in business and tort litigation. Our experience includes energy, environmental and toxic torts, antitrust and anti-competitive practices, employment and labor disputes, as well as product liability cases. Since our founding in 1997, we have consistently delivered successful results in complex cases, building a reputation for excellence, efficiency, and outstanding client service.

Appellate Litigation

Hicks Thomas LLP specializes in appellate litigation, adeptly handling appeals of trial court rulings. Our skilled lawyers anticipate, prepare for, and successfully argue appeals, ensuring favorable outcomes in higher courts.
